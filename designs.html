The Portfolio

Beyond the build:
the EcoDome™ advantage.

This changes not just how houses are built, but how they are lived in. The airformed shell offers more than safety: architectural freedom and efficiency that box construction cannot match.

i.

Architectural Freedom.
Walls without limits.

The exterior shell carries 100% of the load, so the interior is a blank canvas. It can even be built above or below ground.

No Load-Bearing Walls

Tear down the barriers. Create sweeping, cathedral-like open floor plans, or intimate, partitioned spaces. Your interior layout is dictated by your lifestyle, not by where a column needs to go.

Full Customization

From the placement of skylights to the curvature of the entryway, every EcoDome is bespoke. Whether you prefer a rugged, earth-sheltered aesthetic or a stark, white-walled modernist villa, the shell adapts to your vision.

Stunning Modern Design

Break free from the box. The organic, curvilinear geometry offers a futuristic, soothing aesthetic that stands out in a world of rectangles.

ii.

The Thermal Battery.
Passive efficiency.

An EcoDome works for you. The mass of the concrete shell acts as a thermal battery, regulating temperature naturally.

Passive Heating & Cooling

The structure absorbs heat during the day and releases it slowly at night, and inverts the cycle in winter. That natural regulation keeps your home cool through New England summers, Southwest desert heat, and Mediterranean afternoons alike, and warm through Northern European winters and Rocky Mountain cold snaps. HVAC costs drop by up to 60%, whatever the climate outside.

Ultra-Energy Efficiency

With a continuous, seamless surface area, there are no corners or joints for air to leak out. This airtight envelope makes achieving Net Zero Energy Building (NZEB) standards effortless.

The Indoor Greenhouse

Harness the geometry. The dome's apex is the perfect environment for a skylit atrium or indoor vertical garden, allowing you to grow fresh produce year-round in your own home.

iii.

The Zero-Headache
ownership experience.

We engineer homes for living, not maintaining. The result is a residence that costs less to own, takes less time to build, and gives you more space to live in.

Cosmetic Maintenance, Not Structural

Some owners finish the exterior with tile, timber cladding, or decorative metal. Unlike a conventional home, none of it is structural or keeping the weather out, the composite-reinforced concrete shell does both on its own. Exterior finishes are purely cosmetic, shielding the UV coating that protects the foam beneath. When tiles blow off in a storm or cladding weathers, the home itself is untouched. Plan a cosmetic refresh every 20 to 30 years, a fraction of the paint, siding, and roof cycles that drain conventional homeowners, and nothing structural is ever at stake.

Speed of Construction

Time is money. While neighbors wait 18 months for a bricklayer, your EcoDomeHome shell is inflated and cast in 5 to 7 days (subject to labour, site conditions, and size), with turnkey delivery in as little as 12 weeks. Move in seasons sooner.

Maximized Floor Space

By eliminating thick internal structural walls and utilizing the efficient geometry of the circle, you get more usable living volume per square metre of footprint.
