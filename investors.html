Why now.

For three years, sophisticated capital has been concentrated in one place: the AI race. Every fund, every model, every announcement obsolete within months — replaced by the next iteration of the same idea. The returns may eventually arrive. The exhaustion of staring at the same thesis at higher and higher prices has already arrived.

EcoDomeHomes and M45 are not a counter-argument to AI. They are a counter-cycle — for investors who have already taken their AI position and are now looking for the other half of a balanced book.

— The other side of the ledger

Tangible. Physical. Permanent.

An asset that exists when the power is off. A 500-year service life. An IP filing, not a sentiment. Three independent commercial markets, each measured in hundreds of billions to trillions globally. A welcome diversification from a portfolio of intangibles whose value is predicated on user adoption curves that may or may not happen.

The opportunity.

The global residential construction industry has not had a structural innovation in over a century. Conventional homes are assembled from thousands of weak joints, designed for a 50–80 year service life, and rebuilt every generation at enormous economic and carbon cost.

EcoDomeHomes addresses this directly through airformed concrete shell construction — a process that produces a continuous, joint-free structural envelope in a fraction of the time and at competitive cost. The result is a residence engineered for a 500-year service life that is hurricane-rated, fireproof, and operates at 50–75% lower energy demand than conventional homes.

— Market scale

One technology, three trillion-dollar verticals.

The same shell construction system serves residential housing, agricultural infrastructure, and commercial / defence — three independent markets, each measured in the hundreds of billions to trillions globally, each presently underserved by disaster-resilient, energy-efficient, fast-built alternatives.

The technology.

The structural core is an airformed pneumatic shell — sprayed with high-strength concrete, reinforced with advanced composite materials, and insulated with continuous closed-cell foam. The process eliminates the cost drivers and failure points of conventional construction in a single integrated build sequence.

Technical details — including the composite reinforcement methodology, optional shielding layers, and proprietary build sequences — are protected under filed intellectual property and available to qualified parties under NDA.

Traction & partnerships.

The platform has secured formal positioning within Portugal's institutional innovation network and built a multi-region supply chain to deliver against an active project pipeline across Europe and the United States.

Engineering & research partnership

An active partnership with CRIA — the entrepreneurship and innovation division of the University of Algarve — provides ongoing research, validation, and engineering review of the construction methodology. The partnership delivers academic-grade testing and continuous engineering iteration, anchoring the platform's technical credibility within Portugal's institutional research ecosystem.

Digital Built Accelerator

Memorable Green and EcoDomeHomes were selected for the Digital Built Accelerator, the first construction-sector innovation programme launched by Técnico Venture Lab (the entrepreneurship arm of Instituto Superior Técnico) and Unicorn Factory Lisboa. Part of the European DIGITALbuilt consortium and backed by the European Commission and Portugal's Recovery and Resilience Plan (PRR), the program selected the company's airformed concrete hulls and M45 systems from a competitive international pool of MVP-stage ventures, validating the technology against real-world European deployment needs ahead of an institutional Demo Day.

Markets & roadmap.

EcoDomeHomes and the M45 brands operate across two anchor markets with very different demand drivers — and an expanding international footprint accepted by appointment.

Europe

Anchored in Portugal with a fully domestic supply chain, the European market is the company's operational home. Demand drivers include Mediterranean climate resilience, the rising cost of conventional construction across Southern Europe, NZEB regulatory tailwinds, and a growing buyer appetite for long-lived, low-maintenance residences.

United States

The US market is served by an established domestic supply chain for US builds — keeping cost competitive against conventional US construction while avoiding the logistics, tariff, and lead-time risks of transatlantic material movement. Demand drivers include FEMA-grade hurricane and wildfire resilience, energy independence, and the same long-lived, low-maintenance economics that drive European demand.

International

Builds outside Europe and the United States are accepted by appointment, supported by partner manufacturing as projects warrant.

— What we're building towards

From validated platform to market leadership.

The platform, the IP, the institutional research backing, and the multi-region supply chain are in place. The work ahead is project execution at scale across the three commercial arms — and the relationships, partners, and capital that will support that growth.
