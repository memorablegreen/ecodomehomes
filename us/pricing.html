Pricing FAQ

The questions everyone asks first.

Is this estimate accurate?
It's directionally accurate, based on the same cost model we use to scope real projects. The ±15% range reflects normal variance for site conditions, regulatory differences, and finish selections. Once we evaluate your site and confirm your specifications, you receive a fixed-price written quote that doesn't change.
What's not included in these prices?
The estimate covers everything from architectural design through final inspection of the build level you select. Not included: land purchase, regulatory fees specific to your jurisdiction (permits vary widely), landscaping outside the immediate site footprint, and connection to municipal utilities if not on-site.
Why does location change the price so much?
Three reasons: labor rates differ significantly between Portugal, broader EU, and the US; some materials are more expensive to source outside our Portuguese supply chain; and regulatory compliance varies (US code is more involved than EU code in several respects). Portugal is the baseline because our supply chain is fully domestic here.
Can I finance this like a normal home?
Yes, EcoDomeHomes residences are permitted, inspected, and insured like conventional homes. They qualify for standard residential mortgages in Portugal and the US. We can share our experience with specific lenders during your initial planning call.
How much do I need to put down to reserve a build slot?
Typical project deposit is 10% of the contract price, due on signature. This secures your slot in the build calendar and funds the design and engineering phase. Subsequent payments are tied to construction milestones.
What if I want something not in the calculator?
The calculator covers about 80% of typical projects. For larger compounds, unusual site conditions, fully custom architecture, or commercial-adjacent residential (e.g., a home with an attached agricultural structure), request a tailored quote, we'll respond within one business day.
