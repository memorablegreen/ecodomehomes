Permanent infrastructure, airformed in weeks.

Why now.

For three years, sophisticated capital has concentrated in one place: the AI race, every model obsolete within months. The returns may eventually arrive. The exhaustion of the same thesis at higher and higher prices already has.

EcoDomeHomes and M45 are not a counter-argument to AI. They are a counter-cycle, for investors who hold their AI position and want the other half of a balanced book.

The Other Side of the Ledger

Tangible. Physical. Permanent.

An asset that exists when the power is off. A 500-year service life. An IP filing, not a sentiment. Three independent markets, each measured in hundreds of billions to trillions. Diversification from a book of intangibles priced on adoption curves that may or may not happen.

The opportunity.

Residential construction has not had a structural innovation in over a century. Conventional homes are assembled from thousands of weak joints, last 50 to 80 years, and are rebuilt every generation at enormous economic and carbon cost.

EcoDomeHomes answers this with airformed concrete shell construction: a continuous, joint-free envelope built in a fraction of the time at competitive cost. The result is a hurricane-rated, fireproof residence engineered for 500 years that uses 50 to 75% less energy than a conventional home.

Market Scale

One technology, three trillion-dollar verticals.

The same shell serves residential, agricultural, and commercial / defense: three independent markets, each in the hundreds of billions to trillions globally, each underserved by disaster-resilient, energy-efficient, fast-built alternatives.

The technology.

The structural core is an airformed pneumatic shell: sprayed with high-strength concrete, reinforced with advanced composite reinforcement, and insulated with continuous closed-cell foam. One integrated build sequence removes the cost drivers and failure points of conventional construction.

Technical details, including the composite reinforcement methodology, optional shielding layers, and proprietary build sequences, are protected under filed IP and available to qualified parties under NDA.

Markets & roadmap.

EcoDomeHomes and the M45 brands operate across two anchor markets with very different demand drivers, plus an expanding international footprint by appointment.

Anchor Market

Europe

Anchored in Portugal with a fully domestic supply chain, the European market is the company's operational home. Demand drivers include Mediterranean climate resilience, the rising cost of conventional construction across Southern Europe, NZEB regulatory tailwinds, and a growing buyer appetite for long-lived, low-maintenance residences.

Anchor Market

United States

An established domestic supply chain serves US builds, keeping cost competitive against conventional US construction while avoiding the logistics, tariff, and lead-time risk of transatlantic shipping. Demand drivers include FEMA-grade hurricane and wildfire resilience, energy independence, and the same long-lived, low-maintenance economics as Europe.

Expanding Footprint

International

Builds outside Europe and the United States are accepted by appointment, supported by partner manufacturing as projects warrant.

What We're Building Towards

From validated platform to market leadership.

The platform, the IP, the institutional research backing, and the multi-region supply chain are in place. The work ahead is project execution at scale across the three arms, with the partners and capital to support that growth.
