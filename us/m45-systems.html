Engineering envelope

Outside the limits of conventional construction.

M45 Systems structures operate in environments where steel-and-concrete construction is uneconomical or non-functional. The same shell technology that builds homes scales to span 100 meters.

i.

Structural span

Single-pour spans from 8 ft up to 330+ ft diameter, with silo configurations up to 11 stories. No internal columns required.

ii.

Disaster resistance

Engineered to FEMA P-361 criteria. 250 mph wind resistance. IBC Type II fire rating. Continuous seismic load path.

iii.

Subterranean capacity

Up to 30 ft burial depth with 2,000 PSF overburden capacity. Engineered for hardened, underground, and earth-bermed applications.

iv.

Specialized layers

Optional integrated EMP shielding (Faraday cage layer), thermal-mass HVAC reduction, and acoustic engineering for sensitive interiors.
