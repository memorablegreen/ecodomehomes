CRIA Acceptance

Memorable Green and EcoDomeHomes.com Join CRIA to Accelerate Advanced Construction in the Algarve

The momentum for sustainable, next-generation infrastructure is continuing to build. Following our recent integration into Lisbon's tech ecosystem, we are taking a massive step forward to solidify our presence right here in our home region of the Algarve.

We are incredibly proud to announce that Memorable Green and EcoDomeHomes.com have officially been accepted into CRIA (the Division of Entrepreneurship and Technology Transfer of the University of the Algarve).

As an elite regional division focused on accelerating technology-based companies and bridging academic research with industrial application, CRIA represents the premier hub for innovation in southern Portugal. This relationship places our specialized building platforms right at the cutting edge of the regional technology transfer pipeline.

Positioning Portugal as a Global Leader in R&D

Our vision extends far beyond local construction; we are establishing a world-class R&D hub that will position Portugal as a global leader in sustainable, high-performance housing. By combining eco-friendly, continuous concrete shells with advanced electromagnetic shielding, we are developing structures engineered for the future of global infrastructure.

As part of our expanding research pipeline, we will be conducting rigorous testing of advanced EMP (electromagnetic pulse) shielding. This initiative will utilize our own patented methodology alongside cutting-edge technologies developed by fellow pioneering startups across Portugal. By creating a collaborative testing ecosystem, we are uniting the best of domestic tech innovation to deliver truly impervious, grid-independent structures.

Bridging Academic Research and Commercial Sourcing

Our building systems, which replace traditional legacy framing with seamless concrete shells, rely heavily on structural optimization, thermodynamics, and advanced materials science. Joining CRIA gives us a direct line to the University of the Algarve's research facilities, enabling us to continuously test and refine our sustainable, climate-resilient building technologies.

This collaboration aligns perfectly with our recent commitment to a 100% Portuguese domestic supply chain. By collaborating with CRIA's vast network of regional actors, we are uniquely positioned to:

  • Advance Materials SourcingPartner with local entities to analyze, refine, and deploy local aggregate and mineral rock composite materials.
  • Optimize Structural EfficiencyWork alongside regional engineering and technology disciplines to further validate our low-carbon building systems.
  • Accelerate Agritech DeploymentLeverage local agricultural expertise to expand our specialized M45 Agritech division, providing hyper-resilient, climate-insulated structures designed for regional farming, logistics, and eco-communities.
“Navigating a highly complex, advanced construction pipeline requires a balance of innovative physical engineering and local institutional trust. Joining CRIA allows us to root our commercial operations deeply within the academic and technological bedrock of the Algarve. With our founding partner Carlos Justino already intimately tied to this ecosystem, this is a natural, powerful step toward delivering permanent, disaster-proof structures across the region.” Christopher Garner, Founder

A Shared Vision for Regional Economic Growth

For over two decades, CRIA has been a critical driver of economic diversification in southern Portugal, boasting an incredible track record of launching and sustaining high-growth tech ventures. By welcoming our high-performance construction platform into its fold, CRIA is actively supporting a paradigm shift in how infrastructure is built in the Algarve and beyond.

Through this alignment, we gain access to crucial regional support mechanisms:

  • Intellectual Property & Licensing GuardrailsEnsuring our proprietary seamless construction processes, EMP shielding methods, and structural designs are robustly protected and optimized for international scale.
  • Regional Strategic Joint VenturesCreating immediate collaborative links with regional developers, agricultural innovators, and municipalities that desperately need climate-impervious infrastructure.
  • Deep Institutional CredibilityStanding shoulder-to-shoulder with the top researchers and tech-driven minds in the country.

The Foundation is Set

We are living through a unique moment where climate volatility demands smarter, stronger buildings. By embedding our operations within the University of the Algarve's premier innovation hub, we are ensuring that our permanent, energy-efficient concrete shells are backed by rigorous scientific support, trusted local networks, and a shared passion for regional economic progress.

The era of fragile, high-emission legacy building is coming to an end. Together with CRIA and our domestic startup partners, we are building a more resilient, globally impactful future right here in the Algarve.
