Privacy & Cookie Policy

What We Collect

EcoDomeHomes.com uses a small number of analytics services to understand how visitors use the site and to improve the experience. These are:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — collects anonymous page-view and event data such as pages visited, session duration, and general geographic region. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Microsoft Clarity — records anonymised session replays and heatmaps so we can see how visitors interact with the site. Clarity automatically masks sensitive input fields.
  • Vercel Analytics — collects lightweight, privacy-friendly traffic metrics (page views, web vitals) with no cookies required.

Cookies & Consent

All analytics cookies — including those set by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity — require your explicit opt-in consent via our cookie banner, powered by Klaro. No tracking cookies are placed until you actively agree.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clicking "Manage consent" in the cookie banner, which is always accessible at the bottom of the page.

Vercel Analytics does not use cookies and runs without requiring consent.

Data Storage

Analytics data is processed and stored by the respective service providers:

  • Google (GA4) — data is processed under Google's data processing terms and may be stored in data centres worldwide.
  • Microsoft (Clarity) — data is processed under Microsoft's privacy terms.
  • Vercel — traffic metrics are processed under Vercel's privacy policy.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Analytics data is used exclusively to understand site usage and improve our content and services.

Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure — request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing of your personal data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at EcoDomeHomes@memorablegreen.com.
