Frequently asked

The questions everyone asks.

How much does an EcoDomeHomes home actually cost?
It depends on size, configuration, and finish. In Portugal, starting prices are €115k for the Family (80 m², 2-bed), €155k for the Coastal, and €275k for the Compound, with finished tiers shown in the pricing tool. You get a fixed-price quote within two weeks.
Where do you build? Can you build at my site?
Our primary markets are Europe and the United States; builds elsewhere are by appointment. We evaluate your site as part of the design conversation and tell you honestly if it's a good fit.
Is this really 12 weeks? What's the catch?
Yes, 12 weeks from site-ready foundation to finished home. Design and engineering add about 4 weeks up front, and permitting varies by location. The build itself is the fast part.
Will it look like a "weird dome house"?
Only if you want it to. The shell is the canvas and the finish is yours, from minimalist plaster to stone cladding to a traditional roof. Inside, they're conventional rooms with conventional finishes.
Can I finance it like a normal home?
Yes. Our homes are permitted, inspected, and insured like any house, and qualify for standard residential mortgages in Portugal and the US.
What about resale value? Is this hard to sell later?
They appreciate at the same rate or better than conventional homes nearby, with the lowest operating costs and longest service life of any home. The math is straightforward.

Ready to start the conversation?

The first step is a 30-minute call about your site, your goals, and the configuration that fits.
