Selected for the Inaugural Digital Built Accelerator

Memorable Green and EcoDomeHomes.com selected to join the first-ever construction-sector innovation programme launched by Técnico Venture Lab and Unicorn Factory Lisboa.

The momentum is shifting. As we continue to commercialize our high-performance, low-carbon building systems across Europe, we have hit a monumental new milestone.

We are incredibly proud to announce that Memorable Green and EcoDomeHomes.com have been selected to join the Digital Built Accelerator.

This is a historic appointment: it is the first-ever innovation and acceleration program dedicated entirely to the construction sector launched by the Técnico Venture Lab (the entrepreneurship arm of the historic Instituto Superior Técnico) and Unicorn Factory Lisboa. Integrated into the broader European DIGITALbuilt consortium and backed by the European Commission and the Portuguese Recovery and Resilience Plan (PRR), this acceleration platform was explicitly built to find and scale the technologies that will drag the traditional construction sector into a sustainable digital era.

Out of a highly competitive pool of international applicants with minimum viable products (MVPs), our airformed concrete dome hulls and M45 systems were chosen as a definitive solution for the future of resilient infrastructure.

Why This First-of-Its-Kind Programme Matters

The construction sector is one of the most vital legacy industries globally, but it is also historically one of the slowest to innovate. The Digital Built Accelerator targets this exact friction point by focusing on three priority pillars:

ConstructTech. Utilizing AI, automation, and advanced digital platforms to eliminate planning inefficiencies, enhance tracking, and optimise project management.

ConTech. Deploying on-site optimization tools like robotics, modular construction, and prefabrication to streamline real-world deployment.

New Materials. Engineering low-carbon, super-resilient, and environmentally friendly alternatives, such as alternative concrete formulations, to reduce the built environment's carbon footprint.

Our building philosophy perfectly bridges these domains. By substituting multi-layered, climate-vulnerable framing with seamless, continuous concrete shields, we are providing a structural material shift that achieves immediate carbon reduction, high energy efficiency, and permanent climate resilience.

Through this program, our team will work alongside top-tier industry experts, structural engineers, and corporate partners to align our systems with the pressing, real-world deployment needs of the broader European market.

“Securing a spot in this inaugural cohort is validation of what we've known all along: the industry recognizes that legacy building practices are broken. Joining forces with the academic brilliance of Instituto Superior Técnico and the hyper-growth ecosystem of Unicorn Factory Lisboa gives us the exact launching pad we need to scale our disaster-proof communities across Europe.” Christopher Garner, Founder

Scaling Infrastructure via Europe's Top Tech Ecosystem

Operating out of Lisbon's booming innovation network, which recently expanded its dedicated physical spaces like the newly opened Engineers Hub, this accelerator opens up deep structural pathways for our operations:

Direct Market Validation. We will be validating our technology in real-context industrial scenarios, proving our cost-parity models directly to massive strategic partners.

Deep Institutional Networks. Integration into the combined ecosystems of Técnico Venture Lab and Unicorn Factory Lisboa provides direct, daily connectivity to strategic partners, angel investors, and industrial infrastructure giants.

Digital Optimization. Alongside our recently announced Founding Partner, Carlos Justino, whose AI ventures work hand-in-hand with these exact innovation hubs, we are fully embedding advanced predictive analytics and workflow automation into our building pipeline.

The Road to Demo Day

The intensive acceleration phase is currently underway, designed to rapidly scale our commercial readiness. It will all culminate in an exclusive Demo Day, where we will present our fully optimised structural deployment strategy to a global room of institutional investors, master-planned developers, and European policymakers.

The future of housing isn't just a vision on paper anymore, it is being accelerated by the highest economic and academic institutions in the region. The legacy era is drawing to a close, and we are proud to be leading the charge right from the heart of Lisbon.
